Tafsilu khomsatin Waisyrina lazim kullu Mu kallafin faa Haqqiq waghtanim hum Adamu Idrizisun Nuhun Huduma Saleh Waa Ibrahimu Kullu Muttabaa

Luthun Waa Ismailu Ishaku kadza Ya’qubu Yusufu Waa Ayubuh Tadza Syu’aibu Haruna Wal Musa Wal Yasa Zulkifli Daudu Sulaimanut Tabba

Walmalakulladzi Billa Abin Waum La akla la Syurba Wala Nauma Lahum Tafsilu Asri minhumu Jibrilun Mikalu Isrofilu Izroilu

Abuhu Abdullahi Abdul Mutolib Wa hasyimun Abdu manafin yantasib Wa Umuhu aminatu zuhriyyah ardhoathu halimatus sa’diyyah

Mauliduhubimma kattal amiinah Wafatuhu biitooybatal Madiinah Atamma qoblal wahyi Arbinaa Waa Umruhu qod jawazas sitiina

Waa Sab atun aulaaduhu faminhumu salasatum mina dzukuri tufhamu Qosim wa Abdullahi wahwa toyyibuu Waa thohirun Bidzayni dzayulaqqobu

Atahu ibrohiimu min sariyyah faummuhu mariiyyatul qibtiyyah waghoiro ibrohiima min khodiijah hum sitatun fakhudz bihim waliijah

Wa arbaum minal inaasi tudzkaru ridwaanu robbi liljami’i yudzkaru fatimatuzzahro’u ba’luha ‘ali wabnahumas sibthoni fadluhum jali

Fazaynabun waba’daha ruqoyyah waummu kulsummin zakat rodiyyah antisi niswatin wafatul musthofa khuyyirna fakhtarnan nabiyyal muqtafa

A’isyatun wa hafshotun wa saudah shofiyyatun maymunatun wa romlah hindun wa zaynabun kadza juwairiyyah

Lilmu’miniina ummahatun mardiyyah hamzatu ammuhu wa abbasun kadza ammatuhu shofiyyatun dzatuh tidza wa qobla hijrotin nabiyyil isro

Min makkatil laila liqudsi yudro waba’dal isroi uruju lissama hatta roannabiyyil robban kallama min ghoiri kaifin wanhinshorin waftarodh

Alaihi khomsan ba’da khomsiina farodh wa ballagol ummata bil isro’i wafardi khomsatim bilam tiro’i qod faza shiddiqum bitasdiiqilahu

Wabil urujis shidqu wafa ahlahu wa hadzihi aqidatum mukhtashoroh walil awami sahlatum muyyassaroh nazimu tilka ahmadul marzuqi

Mayyantami lisshodiqil masduuqi walhamdulillahi washolla sallama alannabiyyi khoiri man qod allama wal ali washohbi wakulli mursyidi

Wakulli man bikhoiri hadyi yaqtadi wa as alul kariima ikhlashol amal wanaf’a kulli man biha qodisy tagol abyatuha maizun biaddil jumali

Tarikhuha li hayyu ghurrin jumali sammaituha aqidatal awami min wajibin fiddini bittamami wa sholallahu ala sayyidina muhammad wa ala alihi washohbihi wassalam
